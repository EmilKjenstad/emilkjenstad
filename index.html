biography

Hello,

I'm Emil Kjenstad, a software developer based in the United States with Norwegian roots. My journey into software development began in high school, where I dabbled in creating web applications for fictional video game franchises. As I delved deeper into coding, I discovered my passion for it and realized I wanted to turn it into a career.

After graduating from the University of Pittsburgh with Summa Cum Laude honors, I faced a decision: enter the industry or pursue a master's degree. Reflecting on my educational journey, I recognized a need to enhance my theoretical skills, so I opted for a master's degree from the University of Oslo.

Today, I'm eager to apply my skills in a practical setting, gain valuable experience, and continue refining my software development expertise. I'm excited about what the future holds and the paths my career may take.
